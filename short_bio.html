Bartosz Zieliński is an Associate Professor at the Jagiellonian University in Kraków. He received M.Sc. degree from Jagiellonian University in 2007, Ph.D. from the Polish Academy of Science in 2012, and Habilitation from the Wrocław University of Science and Technology in 2022, all in Computer Science. He is an ELLIS member, and he frequently serves as a reviewer in major computer science conferences and journals. His research interests include computer vision, deep learning, interpretable machine learning, and embodied artificial intelligence.
